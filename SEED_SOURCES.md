# Seed sources — handbook sections used for the 20 starter questions

Source of truth: **PennDOT Sổ Tay Tài Xế / PA Driver's Manual, PUB 95
(4-21), bản tiếng Việt.**

These 20 questions were seeded from the handbook's topic chapters and their
chapter review-question / answer-key sections. Wording was kept close to the
handbook and lightly rewritten for app readability. No legal rules were
invented; PA-specific facts (BAC limits, point system, headlight/wiper law,
distances) reflect the manual.

> Note: chapter numbers below are approximate pointers to the relevant
> handbook sections for editor verification. Confirm exact page/chapter
> against your copy of PUB 95 (bản tiếng Việt) before publishing.

## By topic

### 1. Đèn tín hiệu, biển báo và vạch kẻ đường (q-001 … q-005)
- Handbook chapter on **signals, signs, and pavement markings**.
- Covered: STOP sign (octagon), yellow signal meaning, yellow diamond
  warning signs, solid yellow no-passing line, flashing red signal.

### 2. Kỹ năng lái xe (q-006 … q-010)
- Handbook chapter on **driving skills / safe driving**.
- Covered: 4-second following distance, PA wipers-on→headlights-on law,
  skid recovery (steer into the skid), hydroplaning recovery, high-beam
  dimming distance (500 ft oncoming).

### 3. Nhường đường, đỗ xe, an toàn (q-011 … q-015)
- Handbook chapter on **right-of-way, parking, and safety**.
- Covered: yielding to emergency vehicles, school-bus red-flashing-lights
  stop rule, no-parking within 15 ft of a fire hydrant, parking within
  12 in of the curb, 4-way stop right-of-way.

### 4. DUI, nhắn tin khi lái xe, và các quy định liên quan (q-016 … q-020)
- Handbook chapter on **alcohol, drugs, and the law**.
- Covered: 0.08% BAC (21+), 0.02% zero-tolerance (under 21), PA texting
  ban, implied-consent license suspension, 6-point special written exam.
